Validation rules

Contract requirements

Merchant authorization

The connected wallet must equal the voucher campaign’s merchant address.

Customer ownership

The customer must hold at least one ERC1155 voucher token for the selected voucher ID.

Contract state

VoucherRedemption must not be paused when the merchant uses the voucher.
